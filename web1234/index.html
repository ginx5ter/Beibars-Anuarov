Astana Hub

International technopark for IT startups with created conditions for the free development of Kazakhstani and international technological companies. Technopark programs are available for Kazakhstani and international representatives of start-up field and these programs recently started to be held online. There is a special tax regime for Astana Hub participants.

AstanaIT University

Astana IT University is the leading center of competencies for digital transformation in Central Asia, the aim of which is to train highly qualified specialists in the field of digital economy based on interdisciplinary technologies and ensure digital transformation through training, research and successful innovation.

EXPO Congress Center

The congress center with a total area of 43,200 square meters has 3 floors, as well as an underground parking lot, which is designed for 179 m/place. The height of the building is 29 meters. The five-star Hilton Astana Hotel and Mega Silk Way shopping and entertainment center are located within easy reach.

The Congress Center opened on June 10, 2017 in Nur-Sultan. It has a wide range of opportunities for holding international forums, conferences, official events, as well as sport and cultural events.

EXPO International Exhibition Center

Multifunctional business arena for major international and national exhibitions, congress events and other large-scale events. The total area is 23 300 square meters. There is an open parking space with a total area of 6 200 square meters or 120 m/space that can be used for exhibitions.

The exhibition pavilion is equipped with modern communication facilities, engineering networks and convenient access roads. The technical data of the pavilion allows developers to install both standard and exclusive exhibition stands.

Hilton Astana

Hilton Astana is an ultra-modern hotel on the territory of the futuristic EXPO-2017 exhibition complex. Enjoy high-quality service and thoughtful amenities such as a spa and wellness center, rooftop bar, fitness center, indoor pool, Executive Lounge and spacious event spaces. We are located a short drive from the city center, offices of large companies, government and government agencies in Kazakhstan and from Astana International Airport (TSE).

Astana International Financial Centre

Astana International Financial Centre (AIFC) plays a pivotal role in positioning itself as a global centre for business and finance, connecting the economies of the Central Asia, the Caucasus, EAEU, West China, Mongolia, Middle East and Europe.

The International Center for Green Technologies and Investment Projects NJSC

One of the anchor initiatives in the field of green technologies on the territory of the EXPO Business district. The mission is to promote green technologies and business support to improve the competitiveness of the economy, the quality of life of the population and reduce the negative impact on the environment.
